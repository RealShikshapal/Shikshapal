What is ShikshaPal?

ShikshaPal is an AI-powered learning copilot for Indian K-12 education. It helps students understand concepts, practice questions, clear doubts, and track progress with support for parents, teachers, and schools.

Is ShikshaPal aligned with CBSE and NCERT?

Yes. ShikshaPal focuses on CBSE and NCERT-aligned learning, including concept explanations, adaptive practice, assessment support, and explainer lessons for middle-school students.

Who is ShikshaPal for?

ShikshaPal is built for students who need concept clarity, parents who want safe AI help, teachers who need planning and grading support, and schools that want learning analytics.
